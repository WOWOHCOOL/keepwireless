My Journey

2019

Entered Wireless Charging Industry

Started as a hardware engineer focusing on PCB design and power conversion efficiency for major ODM factories in Shenzhen.

2021

Discovered the Certification Crisis

Realized that over 60% of products claiming "Qi2 certified" either had fake certificates or hadn't completed proper WPC testing. Began systematic verification.

2023

Launched KeepWireless

Created this platform to bridge the gap between B2B buyers and verified quality products. Started publishing lab test results and sourcing guides.

2026

Serving Global B2B Clients

Now helping businesses in USA, UK, Germany, and Canada source authentic Qi2/MagSafe products with confidence.
