Common Questions

Frequently Asked

What is your minimum order quantity (MOQ)?

MOQ varies by product. Some items available from 50 units, others require 500+. Contact me with your requirements for specific details.

How do you verify Qi2 certification?

I check products against the official WPC (Wireless Power Consortium) database, verify certificate numbers, and can arrange factory audits when needed.

Do you offer custom branding (OEM/ODM)?

Yes, I can connect you with factories offering OEM and ODM services. I can also oversee the development process to ensure quality.

What countries do you serve?

Primary markets: USA, UK, Germany, Canada. I also work with clients in other regions. English and Chinese languages supported.

How long does sourcing take?

Typical timeline: 1-2 weeks for product identification, 2-4 weeks for sample evaluation, 4-6 weeks for production. Exact timing depends on requirements.

What are your payment terms?

Standard: 30% deposit, 70% before shipment. Other arrangements possible for established clients. Secure payment methods accepted.
